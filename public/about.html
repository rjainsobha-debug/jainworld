About

JainWorld is being built as a practical digital home for Jain life.

The platform brings together Jain learning, literature, temple discovery, food guidance, calendar support, audio, news, resources, and community pathways in a simple structure.

What JainWorld covers

Jain texts, education, temples, food practices, festival reminders, audio, blogs, news, resources, and community support.

How the platform works

JainWorld combines static pages for speed with a live content layer for listings, updates, and structured collections.

What JainWorld does not claim

We do not claim to replace religious teachers, institutions, official notices, or legal advice. Time-sensitive details should always be verified at the source.
