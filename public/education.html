Where should a beginner start?

Begin with simple lessons on Jain values, Namokar Mantra, food discipline, temple etiquette, and the meaning of Ahimsa in daily life.

Is this page useful for children and families?

Yes. JainWorld is being shaped for students, children, parents, and families who want a calmer and easier entry into Jain learning.

Can serious students use it too?

Yes. The goal is to support both beginner-friendly learning and deeper study through literature, philosophy, and advanced learning paths.
