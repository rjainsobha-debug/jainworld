Editorial Policy

How JainWorld approaches review, attribution, and religious sensitivity

JainWorld aims to present useful, respectful, and well-structured public information while clearly separating editorial content from external sources.

Source attribution

Where appropriate, JainWorld notes the source of facts, links, and updates. News cards and resource cards should clearly distinguish external sources from JainWorld editorial text.

Religious sensitivity

JainWorld aims to present religious, cultural, and sect-sensitive topics with care, avoiding sensational language and overclaiming.

Corrections

If you notice factual errors, broken official links, metadata issues, or translation problems, please use the corrections page so the issue can be reviewed.

Editorial vs external content

JainWorld editorial summaries are not the same as original news reporting or official government or temple announcements. Visitors should verify time-sensitive details at the source.
