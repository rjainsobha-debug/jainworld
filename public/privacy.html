Privacy

How JainWorld handles your information

This page explains what information JainWorld collects, why it is collected, and how visitors can request changes or deletion.

What we collect

Community forms may collect name, mobile number, email, city, country, interests, contribution details, and optional WhatsApp consent.

How it is used

Information is used to review community requests, respond to relevant inquiries, and improve JainWorld services. Mobile numbers and email addresses are not intended for public display.

Analytics

JainWorld uses site analytics to understand traffic and improve user experience. These tools do not replace privacy obligations.

Deletion or correction requests

To request deletion or correction of submitted information, contact JainWorld using the contact placeholders and mention the relevant submission details.
