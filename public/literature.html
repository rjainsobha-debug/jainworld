Is this page only for scholars?

No. JainWorld literature is meant to support both first-time readers and serious learners, with simpler entry points where possible.

Can families use this for children?

Yes. Stories, devotional reading, and simple summaries can help children and families approach Jain literature in a comfortable way.

What if I do not know where to start?

Start with stories, values, and short summaries, then gradually move to sutras, philosophy, and deeper study.
