# JainWorld Content Quality Guidelines

JainWorld content should remain respectful, cautious, and easy for families, students, and seniors to trust.

## Core Rules

- Do not invent temple timings, addresses, phone numbers, trust contacts, maps, or official stay details.
- Do not publish scholarship deadlines, eligibility rules, or government benefits unless they are verified from official sources.
- Do not add copyrighted bhajan, aarti, stavan, or pravachan lyrics unless permission is clearly available.
- Do not upload copyrighted audio or claim permission without verification.
- Do not make sectarian claims or present one tradition as the only correct view.
- Do not add medical, legal, or financial advice as fact.

## Religious Content

- Keep religious language respectful and family-friendly.
- Prefer short introductions, study guides, and cautious summaries over long unverified doctrinal claims.
- Avoid quoting long scripture passages unless the source and usage are clearly safe.
- When a topic may vary by tradition, year, sangh, teacher, or family guidance, say so clearly.

## Temple and Pilgrimage Content

- Use `needs_review` when important temple data is missing or unverified.
- If exact visit details are unavailable, use safe wording such as:
  - `Not available yet`
  - `Please verify locally before visiting`
  - `Source details are being reviewed`
- Encourage users to confirm local timings, dharamshala, bhojanshala, and travel details before visiting.

## Audio and Devotional Content

- Metadata-only records are acceptable when lyrics, transcripts, or audio rights are not confirmed.
- Use `permission_status: needs_review` when permission is unclear.
- Do not imply that public playback rights exist unless they are verified.

## Hindi Content

- Hindi text should use valid UTF-8 Devanagari.
- Hindi content should ideally be reviewed by a human reader before wider publication.
- If good Hindi body content is not available yet, keep English content as fallback but localize the surrounding UI labels.

## Review Status Guidance

- Use `verified` only for general, low-risk content that has been checked carefully.
- Use `curated` for safe editorial summaries and learning guides.
- Use `needs_review` when a record is useful but still needs human verification.
- Use `pending_review` for placeholder or editorial-preview style records that should not be treated as fully trusted.

## Resource and Government Content

- Government, scholarship, and minority-resource content must point users toward official verification.
- Add official URLs only when they are already trusted and verified.
- If an official source is not confirmed, leave the URL blank rather than guessing.

## Search and Ask Readiness

- Titles, summaries, tags, categories, and slugs should be unique and descriptive.
- Add Hindi fields wherever possible so Search and Ask JainWorld can serve bilingual users better.
- Keep wording clear enough for safe search snippets and grounded answer extraction.
