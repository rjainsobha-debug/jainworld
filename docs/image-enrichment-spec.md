# JainWorld Image Enrichment Spec

## Purpose
Support a future image workflow for temple pages, articles, resources, and audio cards without misleading the community or violating copyright.

## Accepted Image Types
- Temple photos
- Article covers
- Resource illustrations
- Audio thumbnails
- Festival graphics

## Suggested Fields
- `image_url`
- `alt_text`
- `caption`
- `source`
- `license_status`
- `review_status`

## Review Rules
- Do not use misleading deity, monk, nun, or ritual imagery.
- Do not publish copyrighted images without permission.
- Temple pages should prefer real documentary photos over generic or generated visuals.
- Generated illustrations should be labeled internally and approved before use.
- Always review alt text and captions for clarity and respect.

## Workflow
1. Add candidate image to review queue.
2. Check origin, license, and editorial suitability.
3. Confirm that the image matches the page subject.
4. Approve, reject, or mark for update.

## Safety Notes
- Avoid sensational or overly decorative religious imagery when a factual photo is more appropriate.
- If no suitable verified image exists, use a neutral default rather than a speculative visual.
